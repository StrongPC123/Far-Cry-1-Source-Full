Compressing with Bink

Discussion

The RAD Video Tools are used to compress your video files into Bink files. These tools are supplied on the Bink SDK CD and can also be downloaded from our web-site at any time. Binking your input video files is usually as easy as highlighting the file and clicking 'Bink'. However, for all the details on compressing your files, use the "Help" button inside the RAD Video Tools window.
