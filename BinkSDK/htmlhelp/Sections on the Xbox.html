Sections on the Xbox

Discussion

Sections are the Xbox's version of overlays. They allow you to link with the Bink static library, but still unload the memory when you aren't actively playing a movie. See the BinkLoad and BinkLoadConverter functions to see how to unload Bink when not in use.
